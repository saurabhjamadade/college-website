Principal's Desk

My Dear Student's,

Indeed it gives me great pleasure to welcome you to UMA Mahavidyalay, PANDHARPUR. I proudly tell you that since the foundation of the college 30 years ago UMA Mahavidyalay has been continuously working hard to provide value based and need based education to students.

The college is fully committed to the entire development of the overall personality of the students. The college offers a wide range of courses Bachelor of Computer Application (B.C.A).

I am extremely happy to inform you that our college has been successfully reaccredited by NAAC Bangalore in the second cycle. Dear friends, may I assure you if you seek admission at UMA Mahavidyalaya, you will certainly be a part of an academic institution, where the need of students is properly understood.

If you want to rebuild our India, we must follow the the Line point agenda suggested by great scientist Dr. Raghunath Mashelkar, it is simply:

  • Child - centred education
  • Woman - Centred family
  • Human - Centred development
  • Knowledge - Centred society
  • Innovation - Centred India

Youth represents the national strength, vitality and energy. YUVAShakti is the real SHAKTI of nation. If properly shaped, the youth can become the champions of our culture. Custodians of our national pride and trustees of the freedom of the country.

In this present age, it is important that every Indian becomes a knowledge worker, be it a farmer, rural woman, a media man and an artist and so on. Knowledge worker is simply one who knows why he is doing, what he is doing. Knowledge without innovation is of no value. With the help of innovation, knowledge is changed into wealth and social good.

"Knowledge will not be only tool in development. Knowledge itself will be development."

I assure you that we are committed to give you above mentioned five point agenda based education, so that your college life would be an enjoyable and memorable experience of your life. Dear friends, I heartily invite you to join our college to make your future bright, make our country the strongest one.

– Dr. Baad D. R.
Principal
UMA Mahavidyalaya, Pandharpur
